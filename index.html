DELIT Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy states how we collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your Personally Identifiable Information in accordance with our mobile application.

What information is contained in our app?

The app contains official contact details of selected officials and officers of the Income Tax Department, from the Delhi NCR region. The details contained are within the domain of public information and may also be contained in the Information Handbook released by the department for public distribution. The information is maintained by nominated officers of the department.

What personal information do we collect from the people that visit our app?

We do not collect information from visitors of our site. We cannot identify nor provide any details of the users that access the application from their mobile device.

What device permissions do we seek from the people that install this app on their mobile device?

Network permissions: this permission is to -

  • Check whether the device is connected via the internet; and
  • Use the Internet from the mobile device in order to update data in the app

Send SMS Permissions:

  • This is for writing file & reading file from device storage. The app data is stored locally so that it can be displayed offline.
  • In addition, this permission is also used for the “Share” option – whereby a user can share the official contact details of an officer with another person via SMS, Whatsapp etc

Send SMS Permissions:

  • This is for sending SMS from device. In directory details screen, the user has the option to send an SMS to the officer

Telephone related permissions:

  • This permission is to check the phone state and
  • for calling the telephone numbers from device. On directory details screen, the user has the option to make a voice call to the officer, or send a message via SMS, Whatsapp etc.

How do we protect your information?

We do not ask the users of the app to register, sign-up, subscribe or sign-in. We never ask for credit card numbers. We do not identify the geo-location of the app users. Users can use this app anonymously. We do not send out any personalised communication to users of this app. The information displayed in this app is not available on any web site. The information is displayed in the app only.

Do we use 'cookies'?

We do not use cookies for tracking purposes

Third-party disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your Personally Identifiable Information. All the information displayed in the app is directly sourced by the Income Tax Department. No data is sourced or sent to any third party entities.

Third-party links

We do not include or offer third-party products or services on our website.
